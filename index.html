Biography

Richard Paul Evans became an author to inspire young children and teenagers to chase their dreams. His hit series, "Michael Vey," inspired many young students to follow their dreams. Just like Michael Vey, Richard also has Tourette's Syndrome. Michael Vey became a best selling series prompting Richard to go around various middle and high schools along with book stores to discuss his passion of writing and inspire others to do so. Seven of his books have become TV Shows such as most recently The Noel Diary and The Miseltoe Inn. His success has come from his motivation to inspire young readers and stemming from his childhood in Salt Lake City, Utah.

The Road Home Series

The Road Home Series and the Michael Vey Series are two of Richard Paul Evan's best selling series. These 2 series are what he is known for! The Michael Vey series has won Best Selling Young Adult Series by New York Times. The Broken Road Series has won the Romantic Times Best Women's Novel. These two series help us understand Richard not only as an author, but as a person and what his passions are. They also help us understand his reasoning as to why he became an author in the first place.
