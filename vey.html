The Michael Vey series tells the tale of a young boy who was born with powers of electricity. Throughout High School, he made a friend who together, would track how much eletricity Michael could create. Eventually, he met a girl who had powers similar to his, which led to a cascade of events that resulted in them meeting with multiple other kids who had different powers of electricity. This Electroclan works together to defeat a crazed mastermind, Dr. Hatch.

On the left, is the first book in the series titled, Rise of the Elgen. The Elgen is the organization run by Hatch. Their goal is to get the Electroclan to join them so that they can control the world and it's electricity. Michael eventually gets so powerful that he turns in to an actual lightning bolt and becomes particles in a cloud by absorbing a lightning bolt. This last and most recent book, "The Traitor," allows us to see what happened to Micael after that event and howmuch his life has changed after the defeat of Hatch and the Elgen.
