Overview

Purpose

The purpose of my website is intended and targeted towards those that want to learn more about the bestselling author, Richard Paul Evans, his book series, "Michael Vey," and other best selling series by him. It is to inform about the author and his past and how he came to be a successful writer of science fiction. The intended audience is teenagers to adults who are passionate about reading and want to learn about great science fiction book series.

Audience

This webiste is intended for those that want to learn more about Richard Paul Evans not just as an author. It is also for those that want to learn about his best selling series and books such as his "Michael Vey" series.

Content

Home page

The Homepage for this website is going to include information about Richard's life. It will include his biography, how many kids he has, where he went to school, etc. It will also include why he became an author and his motivation and drive to continue writing. It will also include a list of his books and fun facts about him and his life. This page will be dedicated to Richard Paul Evans and the many series he made and his biography.

Images for the Home page

Michael Vey

This subpage of the Richard Paul Evans website will inform readers about his best selling series, "Michael Vey." The page will include information about the series along with why it was made and what his inspiration is. It will include a quick summary and overview of the series as well as a description of the best characters. This page will also include information such as why you would want to read this, why he continued writing the series, and if he is still making books on this series today. It will also include facts about the books such as how many he sold, what year it came out, adn things he did because of this series.

Images for the Page 2

The Broken Road

This subpage of this website will include another series written by Richard Paul Evans. This series will overview most of the same information that is covered in the Michael Vey subpage. But this one will include more about his symbolism and other writing techniques he used in this series. This subpage will encompass each book as well as a summary and overview of the series, detail about each character, and why you would wan t to read this series. It will also include facts about the books such as how many he sold, what year it came out and things he did because of this series.
